pelita.messaging.ActorReference.link¶

ActorReference.link(other)¶

Links this actor to another actor and vice versa.

When an actor exits (due to an Exception or because of a normal exit), it sends a StopProcessing message to all linked actors which will then do the same.

This means that it is possible to notify other actors when one actor closes.
