pelita.player.SeededRandomPlayer.time_spent¶

SeededRandomPlayer.time_spent()¶

The approximate amount of time since get_move was called.

Note: Due to delays in network communication and serialisation, this method is not completely reliable concerning the timeout handling. It may however be useful for debugging and as a rough estimation.

Returns :

time_needed : float

time in seconds
