pelita.actors.ServerActor¶

class pelita.actors.ServerActor(**kwargs)¶

Bases: pelita.messaging.actor.DispatchingActor

Actor which is used to handle all incoming requests, assigns each team a RemoteTeamPlayer and registers this with GameMaster.

It also automatically starts a new game whenever two players are accepted.
