pelita.player.RoundBasedPlayer¶

class pelita.player.RoundBasedPlayer(moves)¶

Bases: pelita.player.AbstractPlayer

A Player which makes a decision dependent on the round index in a dict or list. (Or anything which responds to moves[idx].)

Parameters :

moves : list or dict of moves

the moves to make, a move is determined by moves[round]
